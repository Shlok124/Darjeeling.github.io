Famous Places To Visit

Rope Way

Darjeeling Ropeway started in 1986 and is the first cabel car system in India. It was initially set up to cater to the tea gardens at the valleys below which did not otherwise have an easy access. Starting with just one cable car or gondola, it was upgraded to accomodate 16 cable cars having a capacity of six persons.

Toy Train

The Darjeeling Himalayan Railway,also know as DHR or the toy train,is a 2ft gauge railway that runs between New Jalpaiguri and Darjeeling in the Indian state of West Bengal.Built between 1879 and 1881, it is about 88km long.It operates from Darjeeling Railway Station.It's roughly a 2 hour trip from Darjeeling to Ghum and back via Bastasia Loop.

Peace Pagoda

Peace Pagoda, Darjeeling or Darjeeling Peace Pagoda is one of the Peace Pagodas designed to provide a focus for people of all races and creeds to help unite them in their search for world peace. It is located in the town of Darjeeling in the Indian state of West Bengal. Like most of the other Peace Pagodas, it was built under the guidance of Nichidatsu Fujii (1885–1985), a Buddhist monk from Japan and founder of the Nipponzan-Myōhōji Buddhist Order.
